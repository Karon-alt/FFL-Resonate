Our Services

Final Expense

At Family First Life we know that in the days following a loss, there are an enormous amount of details to be attended to. Final expense insurance is specifically created to handle those details.

Mortgage Protection

At Family First Life, we know your home is important, and that’s why we offer mortgage protection insurance. Since 2014, we’ve been working with clients to create personalized policies that offer only the benefits they need. Plus, since we’re not affiliated with a specific insurance company, we have the flexibility to shop around and save you money.

Retirement Plan

Our team understands that everyone has unique financial goals in mind when it comes to retirement. At Family First, we’ve worked with a wide variety of clients to create personalized annuities and retirement plans utilizing universal life policies.

Traditional Life Insurance

You know that the right life insurance policy can ensure your family continues to live comfortably, even after you’re gone. But with scores of different companies and policies out there, the process feel confusing. At Family First Life we’re committed to bringing clarity to the insurance process.
